# Industry Sources & Search Patterns

Use this reference when building search queries in Phase 2. Load this file when you need to expand coverage for a specific industry.

---

## Multifamily / Commercial Real Estate

### Tier 1 Sources (most deal coverage)
- **The Real Deal** — therealdeal.com — NYC, LA, Miami, Chicago, SF editions
- **Multifamily Dive** — multifamilydive.com — national multifamily news
- **Multi-Housing News** — multihousingnews.com — acquisitions, construction loans
- **GlobeSt** — globest.com — national CRE
- **Bisnow** — bisnow.com — local market editions

### Search Patterns
```
site:therealdeal.com "acquired" "multifamily" 2026
site:multifamilydive.com "acquisition" OR "construction loan" 2026
"bridge loan" "apartment" acquisition 2026
"construction loan" multifamily groundbreaking 2026
"value-add" apartment acquisition 2026
```

### Trigger Signals
- Acquisition closed (look for "acquired," "purchased," "closed on")
- Construction loan secured (look for "construction loan," "groundbreaking," "broke ground")
- Fund launch or close (look for "fund," "raised," "close")
- Portfolio disposition (look for "selling," "selloff," "disposition")

## SBA Program Triggers (Always Check)

- **SBA 504 Manufacturing Fee Waiver** — active through Sep 2026; any manufacturer expanding qualifies
- **SBA 7(a) New Lender Approvals** — new lenders entering market signal active borrower pipelines
- **SBA Weekly Loan Reports** — sba.gov/about-sba/sba-newsroom/press-releases-media-advisories
